WHO WE ARE

Australia's Creative Media Company

Diamond Media Group develops premium film, television, broadcasting and digital media projects that entertain audiences, promote destinations and build lasting commercial partnerships.

Built on decades of experience in broadcasting, journalism and production, the company combines creative storytelling with strategic thinking to produce content that connects with audiences both nationally and internationally.

From feature films and television productions to tourism campaigns, podcasts and digital media, every Diamond Media Group project reflects a commitment to innovation, professionalism and exceptional production values.

Led By Experience

Geoff Harrison

Founder & Managing Director

Geoff Harrison has spent almost five decades working across broadcasting, television, journalism, live entertainment and media production.

His career has included commercial radio, television presenting, destination marketing, event production and feature film development, building a reputation for creative thinking, innovation and audience engagement.

Today he leads Diamond Media Group with a vision to produce premium Australian entertainment capable of reaching audiences around the world through cinema, television, streaming and digital platforms.
