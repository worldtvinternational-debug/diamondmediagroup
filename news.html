BREAKING NEWS

Travel Radio Australia Granted Media Accreditation for the ANZDMC Conference

Diamond Media Group is proud to announce that Travel Radio Australia has been officially accredited to attend the Australia and New Zealand Disaster Management Committee (ANZDMC) Conference as accredited media.

Our team will conduct exclusive interviews with emergency management professionals, government agencies, researchers and first responders, providing audiences with behind-the-scenes coverage, conference news and insights into Australia's leading disaster management event.
