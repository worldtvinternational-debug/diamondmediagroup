The Pensioner Gang

Inspired by extraordinary true events, The Pensioner Gang tells the remarkable story of Britain's oldest organised criminal gang. Set against the backdrop of London and Amsterdam in 2008, the film blends suspense, humour and unforgettable characters into a compelling crime drama.

The project is currently in development by Diamond Media Group for international audiences with filmn aing in London and Amsterdam commencing in 2027.

Travel Radio Australia

Travel Radio Australia continues to produce original radio interviews from Australia and around the world, bringing listeners closer to the destinations, personalities and innovations shaping the global tourism industry.

Every month the program features exclusive interviews with tourism leaders, airline executives, cruise operators, destination marketers, travel journalists, broadcasters and industry professionals.

Whether you're a traveller, tourism operator or simply passionate about discovering new places, Travel Radio Australia delivers informative conversations and travel inspiration from across Australia and around the world.
